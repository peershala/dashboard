About Peershala

Our Mission

At Peershala, we encourage peer learning, which is primarily founded on the idea of the Power of Association, so that students can learn crucial skills in small peer groups under the supervision of a mentor who is an authority on that particular topic.

Our Vision

A large demand for inexpensive and simple ways to learn different skills has been created by the online shift and changes in education system the covid and related problems. Peershala is here as a result to solve those inconveniences faced by the individuals.
